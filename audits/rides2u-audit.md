## Audit Summary

Rides2U's website was reviewed from a public-facing perspective.

The site has a clear service offer, strong trust language, and useful proof from riders and parents. The biggest opportunities are speed, page structure, accessibility basics, and simplifying what loads on the homepage.

Main opportunities:

1. Improve page speed and reduce heavy media
2. Make the first screen load faster
3. Clean up scripts and tracking tools that are not needed right away
4. Improve heading structure and accessibility contrast
5. Strengthen page hierarchy so the main message, proof, and app download path are easier to scan
6. Clean up public-facing legal and support details

---

## Messaging Direction

Recommended messaging model: Platform-Led

Messaging Notes:

Rides2U is more than a simple ride booking page. The site speaks to riders, parents, drivers, and partners, and the value comes from the full trust-based transportation system: scheduled rides, vetted drivers, parent/rider visibility, shared and private ride options, and local partner growth.
